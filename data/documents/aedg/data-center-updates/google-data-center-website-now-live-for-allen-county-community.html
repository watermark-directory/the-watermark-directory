Google Data Center Website Now Live for Allen County Community

  • Post date April 27, 2026

In partnership with Google, Allen Economic Development Group (AEDG) has launched a website for the community to learn all about this exciting project. Google’s new data center resource center is officially live at www.allencountydatacenter.com. The website site can also be found at www.aedg.org

A Transparent Hub for the Community

The website was built to be a transparent hub for project facts, timelines, and direct community engagement. As we regularly update the site, feel free to visit the site frequently as a primary source for technical details.

“Google is committed to staying engaged with the community throughout this process, and hopes this site serves as a helpful tool,” said Molly Kocour Boyle, Head of Midwest Data Center Public Affairs for Google. “We plan to keep it updated as more details become available.”
