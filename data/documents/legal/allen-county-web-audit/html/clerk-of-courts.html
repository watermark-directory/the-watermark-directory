Welcome to the Allen County Clerk of Courts


Jennifer McBride
Allen County Clerk of Courts

The Allen County Clerk of Courts is a complex organization that serves the statutory duties relative to the filing, preservation, retrieval and public dissemination of all documents and records pertaining to the Common Pleas Court, Domestic Relations Division and the Third District Court of Appeals.  Our office also offers a wide variety of services pertaining to the Auto Title Division, including titling of motor vehicles.
