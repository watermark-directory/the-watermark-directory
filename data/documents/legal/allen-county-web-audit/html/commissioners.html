Welcome to Allen County Commissioners

Welcome to the Allen County Board of Commissioners’ Home Page. We, the members of the Board, are proud to serve our diverse community, and are dedicated to guiding Allen County into the future with the promise of growth and prosperity. Our region has evolved in recent decades to equally rely upon its resources from industry, agriculture, and service and commercial enterprises. Presenting a broad range of opportunities, excellent schools, sophisticated medical facilities, cultural enrichment, and a friendly population, we believe Allen County is poised for continued new development.
