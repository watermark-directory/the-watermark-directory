Welcome to Auglaize Township


Located in the southeastern corner of Allen County, Auglaize Township is a small, close-knit community with a population of 2,757 (2020 census). Established in 1832, the township has a long history and remains one of only two in Ohio with the name "Auglaize"—the other is in Paulding County.

Whether you’re a resident, visitor, or just passing through, this site is here to keep you informed and connected with what’s happening in the township.
