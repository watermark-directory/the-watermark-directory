January 7, 2026Written by Staff

UNOFFICIAL RESULTS – MAY 5, 2026 PRIMARY

REGISTRATION DEADLINE – April 6, 2026
Voter Registration closes at 9:00 P.M. at the Board of Elections. Voters may register online at VOTEOHIO.GOV until midnight.

ABSENTEE VOTING BEGINS – April 7, 2026
Ballots requested by application start being mailed out. In-person voting at our office begins.

DEADLINE TO SUBMIT APPLICATIONS FOR A BALLOT TO BE MAILED OUT – April 28, 2026

BALLOT RETURN DEADLINE – All absentee ballots must be received by the Board of Elections or placed in the office dropbox by 7:30 p.m. on Election Day (May 5, 2026) to be counted. Ballots that arrive after this time will NOT be counted.

NOTE: Military and overseas voters have different deadlines. Visit our Military and Overseas Voters page for more

Absentee Voting Hours at the Board of Elections

Voting begins the day after the close of registration for the election.
Boards must not be open for early in-person absentee voting on any holiday established by state or federal law.
For the May 5, 2026 primary, the uniform statewide early in-person absentee voting hours are:

Week One, Two, Three

  • 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on each weekday (Monday through Friday)

Week Four of Voting

  •  7:30 a.m. to 7:30 p.m. (Monday)
  •  7:30 a.m. to 8:30 p.m. (Tuesday)
  •  7:30 a.m. to 7:30 p.m. (Wednesday through Friday)
  • 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. (Saturday)
  • 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. (Sunday)
