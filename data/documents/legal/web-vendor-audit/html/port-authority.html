ABOUT THE PROJECT

North Cole Street Improvement Project

In late June/early July, The Port Authority of Allen County will begin construction on a roadway infrastructure project on North Cole Street between SR 115 and Diller Road. 

 

The goals of the project is to improve traffic flow to accommodate development in this area. We want to keep you informed throughout the project.  Sign up below for email updates on the project.
