Electric Aggregation Program Update

Allen County has renewed its electric aggregation contract with Dynegy at a rate of $0.0978/kWh beginning December 2025. Opt-out letters will be mailed to eligible residents in late October. Please contact Valerie at 419-491-1040 with any questions.

On behalf of the Village of Elida, I’m pleased to welcome you to our official website. Whether you’re a resident, business owner, or visitor, we hope this site provides answers to your questions and helps you connect more easily with our community.

If you need further assistance, I encourage you to reach out to our helpful staff — our awesome team is always ready to assist. You’re also welcome to contact our Village Administrator, Dave Metzger, who is a great resource for any questions about village services, community matters, or opportunities in Elida.

Of course, my office is always open to serve both new and longtime residents, as well as businesses interested in calling Elida home. Feel free to call or stop by anytime.

Warm regards,

Mayor Darryl Nichols
