    OUR MISSION

    Allen Metropolitan Housing Authority (AMHA) strives to create a thriving community by offering quality, affordable housing and pathways to self-sufficiency for those we serve.

    OUR VISION

    Transforming lives and communities, one family at a time.

    OUR VALUES

    Accountability

     Upholding fiscal responsibility, transparency, and stewardship to ensure trust and sustainability. 

    Service

    Delivering meaningful value and support to our community with dedication and compassion. 

    Purpose

    Driving our mission with commitment and a focus on empowering individuals and families. 

    Integrity

    Acting with honesty, fairness, and ethical responsibility in all that we do. 

    Respect

    Treating all individuals with dignity, listening attentively, and demonstrating consideration in every interaction. 

    Education

     Providing knowledge, resources, and opportunities to foster growth, self-sufficiency, and empowerment.

    WHAT IS PUBLIC HOUSING?

    Public housing in Lima, Ohio, is managed by Allen Metropolitan Housing Authority (AMHA). This federally funded program helps low-income families, seniors, and people with disabilities find safe and affordable homes.

    ​

    To apply, fill out an application to join the waitlist. When your name reaches the top, AMHA’s Intake Department will contact you for screening. You must pass this process to be approved for housing.

    ​

    To check your application status, call the Intake Department at

    419-228-6065.
