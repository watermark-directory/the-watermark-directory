## The campus's PJM marginal-price / capacity footprint

`watermark.grid.market.derive_pjm_market_scenario` links the facility's first-class total
**`facility_draw`** (`watermark.facility.power`, issue #87 - IT load x PUE) to these figures:

- **Annual consumption** = facility draw x 8760 h x load factor (~0.9, assumption).
- **Annual energy cost** = consumption (MWh/yr) x zonal LMP ($/MWh) -> ~$M/yr (derived).
- **Annual capacity cost** = facility draw (MW) x RPM clearing ($/MW-day) x 365
  -> ~$M/yr (derived). (~348 MW x $269.92/MW-day x 365 ~= **$34M/yr** - the striking
  capacity footprint a single large new load implies at the 2025/2026 clearing price.)
- **Campus vs the queue** = campus MW as a fraction of the large-load queue (GW).

The capacity footprint and the queue framing are the headline. They are a **screening
"bracket, don't overclaim" view, not a settlement, dispatch, or RPM-bidding model** -
the campus's real costs are settled hourly / by delivery year against its actual
contracts, not these annualized averages.

## Known gaps & caveats

- **LMP is connector-sourced; RPM + queue are TRANSCRIBED.** The zonal LMP is pulled live
  from PJM Data Miner 2 (`da_hrl_lmps`) via `watermark.grid.lmp` (#121), keyed by
  `WATERMARK_PJM_API_KEY` (sent only as the `Ocp-Apim-Subscription-Key` header, never cached or
  fixtured); offline replays a committed fixture. The **RPM** clearing price and the
  **large-load queue** remain transcribed at `source: reference`, `confidence: medium`,
  flagged *verify* (regenerate via the **PJM RPM Base Residual Auction reports** and **PJM
  interconnection-queue reporting**). Never fabricate - prefer omission over invention.
- **LMP varies by NODE and HOUR** (LMP = energy + congestion + losses). The committed
  figure is the **AEP-zone day-ahead annual mean** (2025: ~$46/MWh, up from the prior
  ~$35 transcribed placeholder) - a single point, not the campus's bus-specific or
  peak-hour price; congestion is a real component.
- **The RPM clearing price is an RTO-wide CLEARING price, not the campus's contracted
  capacity rate.** The committed 2025/2026 Base Residual Auction figure (~$269.92/MW-day)
  is a major **spike** from the prior 2024/2025 year (~$28.92/MW-day, recorded for
  context) and may not persist; the campus's actual obligation depends on its
  coincident-peak contribution, not nameplate MW.
- **The large-load / interconnection-queue figure is ORDER-OF-MAGNITUDE** (tens of GW)
  and not all queued load is built; the committed ~50 GW value carries an explicit
  "order-of-magnitude, verify" citation and the campus share is illustrative.
- **The campus buys at negotiated wholesale rates, not these public averages.** This is
  the *market-context reference*, not the campus's own bill, and **not a facility
  disclosure** - the only facility-specific input is the first-class `facility_draw`.
