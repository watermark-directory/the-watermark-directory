Tracked individual — **not published to the site**. Kyle C. Brenneman and **Sarah N.
Brenneman** are co-trustees of the **Kyle C. Brenneman Living Trust** (dated 2023-03-30).
Together with the [Keaton A. Brenneman](keaton-brenneman.md) trust, they conveyed **7
parcels** (36-1200-03-001.000–.006) to **Bistrozzi LLC** by Limited Warranty Deed
202508130008300, recorded **2025-08-13** ([deed](../extracted/recorder/202508130008300.deed.yaml)).

**Price withheld:** the deed states only *"valuable consideration"* and the DTE-100
transfer-tax form was produced with the consideration field blank — so what the
Brennemans were paid is not in the record
([seller-land-packets](../extracted/aedg/seller-land-packets.land.yaml)). The land
passed through PAAC options assigned to Bistrozzi (Vorys/Tangeman). A private seller;
tracked as a transaction party, not an accusation.
