## How he appears in the record

Thomas C. Campbell, P.E. (WSP USA Inc., Indianapolis) is the **preparer of record** of the
Storm Water Pollution Prevention Plan (SWP3) for the **Beery Road & Cole Street roundabout /
BOSC Storm Outfall** ([`plans/4091286`](../extracted/plans/4091286.engineering.yaml), Ohio EPA
eDoc 4091286, prepared 2026-04-16).

This is **WSP**'s first appearance as a named corridor design firm in the corpus. WSP is also
referenced (alongside CI Design) as a likely author of the campus architectural sheets in
[`docs/COMPUTE.md`](../../docs/COMPUTE.md) — those data-hall sheets are **not** in hand; this
SWP3 is the first WSP document the corpus actually holds. The SWP3 was prepared **for** the
project's operator/contractor, **George J. Igel & Company** (see
[`christopher-batdorff`](christopher-batdorff.md)).

Low-significance, tracked-only: a design professional named on one stormwater instrument, not a
principal actor. Promote (`expanded_research: true`) if WSP recurs across the corridor record.
