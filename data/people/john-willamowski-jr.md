## How he appears in the record

John R. Willamowski, Jr. is an **Assistant Prosecuting Attorney for Allen County** —
i.e. the County's own counsel — and he appears at two load-bearing points:

- **Originator of the County–Bistrozzi Mutual NDA.** The NDA recitals state that
  "correspondence has been received from Assistant Prosecutor John Willamowski Jr.,
  requesting the Board enter into a Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement with Bistrozzi,
  LLC." The County's own counsel originated the instrument whose §6 became the
  contractual records-withholding engine (≥10 business days' notice to Bistrozzi
  before answering any PRR; disclose only the legal minimum; seek confidential
  treatment).
- **County counsel of record on the Roadwork Development Agreement** (and a notice
  copy recipient), per the RDA notice block.

## Why this matters

The NDA he originated is one of three stacked records-withholding mechanisms in the
deal (NDA §6 / CRA §22 / RDA §9.13), layered on top of the front-end R.C. 4582.58
econ-dev shield. That the *County's own counsel* requested the Board bind itself to
advance-notice-to-the-developer before answering public-records requests is central
to the mandamus narrative.
