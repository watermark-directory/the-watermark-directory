## How he appears in the record

Christopher A. Batdorff (George J. Igel & Company, Inc., Columbus OH) is the named **operator**
contact on the Storm Water Pollution Prevention Plan (SWP3) for the **Beery Road & Cole Street
roundabout / BOSC Storm Outfall** ([`plans/4091286`](../extracted/plans/4091286.engineering.yaml),
Ohio EPA eDoc 4091286, prepared 2026-04-16; engineer of record
[`thomas-campbell`](thomas-campbell.md), WSP USA).

This is **George J. Igel & Company**'s first appearance in the corpus — as the
construction-stormwater **operator** for the roundabout and the BOSC Storm Outfall (the channel
that carries the campus's stormwater to Pike Run). Igel is a distinct contractor from **Turner
Construction**, the campus mass-grading permittee of record (SW1225); the two run separate NPDES
construction-stormwater coverages in the same corridor (see
[`bosc-site-permit-sequence`](../extracted/legal/prr-mandamus/bosc-site-permit-sequence.md)). The
SWP3 also names Igel's SWP3 coordinator **John Powers** and 24-hour emergency contact **Joe Sharp**.
