The mono uppercase part-label that sits above a heading or marks a section start. The signature documentary register of the brand — use it instead of a small bold sans label.

```jsx
<Eyebrow tone="forest">The Watermark directory</Eyebrow>
<h1>The build-out, on the public record.</h1>

<Eyebrow tone="muted">Start here</Eyebrow>
```

Notes
- Always IBM Plex Mono, uppercase, 1.6px tracking, 11px.
- `tone`: `muted` (default), `faint` (quietest), `forest` (when the eyebrow is itself a signal), `ink`.
