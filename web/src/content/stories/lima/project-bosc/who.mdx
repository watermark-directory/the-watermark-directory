<Prose>
Hold the discipline here. Shared agents, shared organizers, a shared
Wilmington mailbox — these are signals of coordination, the plumbing of
common control. They are not, by themselves, proof of who owns or benefits.
The record earns the word *signal*; it has not earned *verdict*.
That distinction is the whole method — and it's why the buyer's name reads
`[verified]` while the link between the shells reads
`[inference]`.
</Prose>

<Callout title="What you can read anywhere now" variant="takeaway">
A **deed** — grantor, grantee, parcels, and the consideration
line, where a blank or nominal price is itself a finding. And a
**Secretary-of-State filing** — the registered agent and
organizer are how you link entities that share nothing else on their face.
Overlaps are leads to verify, not conclusions to publish.
</Callout>
