# docs/legal/

Hand-written legal analysis memos over the corpus. Analysis, not source evidence —
the underlying records live under [`data/documents/legal/`](../../data/documents/legal/)
and their reviewed reads under [`data/extracted/legal/`](../../data/extracted/legal/).

## Contents

| File | What |
|---|---|
| `mandamus-analysis.md` | Analysis behind the PRR mandamus — its §IV timeline events trace to specific commissioners-minutes resolutions. |
| `proponent-analysis.md` | A neutral read of the four proponent submissions (Google, QTS, Data Center Coalition, …) to the Ohio data-center committee. |

## Conventions

Every factual claim must cite a source page in the corpus. Keep the tone neutral and
precise; flag uncertainty rather than overstating. Don't quote machine-derived
transcripts/OCR without verifying against the source.
