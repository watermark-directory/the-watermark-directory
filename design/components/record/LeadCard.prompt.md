An open, unverified thread published in the open for the public to corroborate, answer, or fill in. The "lead, not a verdict" unit — used on investigation-phase site homes and the leads board.

```jsx
<LeadCard
  kind="Open question"
  confidence="unanswered"
  title="Who is behind the Parcel 7 buyer?"
  detail="The deed names a trust with no public principals. Anyone recognize the agent?"
  source="Auditor transfer · 0008300"
  action="Answer this"
  count="open thread"
/>
```

Notes
- The colored left rail + confidence chip together declare standing; never drop the rail.
- `confidence`: low / unanswered (forest) / withheld (muted) / review (under review, amber). The action button is always forest — picking up a lead is the affirmative move.
