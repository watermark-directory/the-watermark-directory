A browse "door" into a part of the record. Open doors carry a mono badge, a count, and a Browse link; locked doors are dashed and explain what unlocks them — the record reveals itself as it fills in.

```jsx
<SectionCard badge="DOC" title="Documents" count="63" />
<SectionCard badge="TML" title="Timeline" locked lockNote="Needs 2 corroborated events" />
```

Notes
- Open badge is forest-tinted; locked badge is neutral with a 🔒 and a dashed border at 0.85 opacity.
- Use in the "record so far" grid on site homes and section hubs.
